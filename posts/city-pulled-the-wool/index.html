For years, we’ve walked these streets, lived our lives, and assumed that everything around us was as it should be. We trusted the neon lights, the towering skyscrapers, the hum of the city as proof of stability, of normalcy. But I am here to tell you that nothing is normal about this city. Not anymore.

You’ve felt it, haven’t you? That creeping unease. That moment where the streetlights flicker, and something moves in the corner of your vision—too fast, too unnatural. The missing people. The buildings that change overnight. The officials who refuse to answer questions. They expect us to ignore the inconsistencies, to laugh at the rumors, to dismiss the anomalies as tricks of perception. But perception is reality, and reality has been warped.

This blog exists to document the undeniable proof—the stories they don’t want you to hear, the sightings that have been covered up, the patterns that lead to something far bigger than any of us can comprehend. I will not be silenced. The people who call me paranoid, who dismiss me as unstable, are the same people who benefit from our ignorance. They need us to stay blind. But I will rip the blindfold off. I will expose the secrets they bury beneath polished reports and shallow reassurances.

Magic was supposed to make the city thrive, technology was supposed to enhance our lives, not twist them into something unrecognizable. But that’s exactly what’s happened. Corporate greed, unchecked experiments, and recklessness have changed the very fabric of our reality. Now, things are leaking through—the kind of things that don’t belong in this world. The kind of things that watch us when we sleep.

I don’t know how deep the corruption runs yet. I don’t know how much time we have before the truth consumes everything. But I do know this: we can’t afford to stay silent any longer. If you’ve seen something—if you’ve experienced something you can’t explain—this is the place for answers. I will piece it together. I will make sense of the chaos.

The city has pulled the wool over our eyes for long enough. But no longer.

Stay vigilant. The truth is coming.
